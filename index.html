    • At the Jeevesington Estates Vineyard, we pride ourselves on getting our fine wines just right so that you can enjoy maximum flavor and quality in your wine experience. In the business of wine-making for nearly 80 years, the Jeevesington Estates have cultivated a unique wine experience that goes beyond traditional vineyards. From dining, to nature to accommodations and activities, our one-stop weekend destination will keep you entertained while also offering plenty of opportunity to get away for more intimate moments.

    Our wines are crafted from the finest home grown Kentucky grapes. We are located in the pristine Applachain Valley, home to ideal wine grape conditions which produce some of the most flavorful and robust wines you'll ever taste.

    Whether you're looking for a crisp, fresh white wine, an aged, robust red, or anything in between, Jeevesington Estates Vineyard is the only stop you need to make on your next journey.
